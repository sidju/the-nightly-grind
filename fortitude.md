# Fortitude

#powers-of-the-blood

### Combat

In combat Fortitude negates 1 damage per level per combat round. Furthermore you
can ignore aggravated damage for as many rounds as you have Fortitude, taking it
as normal damage (reduced by Fortitude as usual).

### Utility

Fortitude allows you to resist even mental assault, when defending treat your
[Mental Ability](mental-ability.md) as 2 higher for every level in Fortitude.
